Trezór Bridge® (Official) | Enhancing™ Connectivity and Security*

Trezór Bridge is a simple software that allows your computer’s web browser to communicate directly with your Trezór hardware wallet. Without this connection, your browser wouldn't be able to detect your Trezór device, making it impossible to manage your cryptocurrencies online through platforms like Trezór Suite or other compatible apps.

Previously, browser extensions handled this communication, but they often came with limitations and potential security risks. Trezór Bridge was created to offer a safer, smoother, and more reliable connection method.
